# 🏀 BasketVision AI

> AI-Powered Basketball Analytics Platform

BasketVision AI is an advanced basketball analytics platform that combines Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, and FIBA LiveStats to help coaches, analysts, clubs, federations, and players gain deep insights from basketball games.

---

# Features

Current Version: **v0.1**

### ✅ AI Basketball Assistant
- Basketball analytics expert
- FIBA LiveStats knowledge
- Advanced basketball statistics
- Offensive & Defensive Rating
- Player development
- Team analytics
